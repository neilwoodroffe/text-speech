Instructions

  • Enter or paste some text in the input box and then press the "Speak" BUTTON to hear it
  • Change the voice by using the dropdown menu
  • You can change the Rate and Pitch of the voice by moving the sliders
  • There are a different variety of voices depending on the browser that you are using
  • Select a voice that you like, matches your nationality or best suits your personality
